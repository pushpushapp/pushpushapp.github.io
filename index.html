PushPush

Send notes, links, SMS messages, and more using OpenFaaS

Welcome to PushPush, the DIY OpenFaaS-based push service! In just a few minutes you can have your own, free push service running in your browser and on your Android phone that allows you to push notes, links, SMS messages, and tasks (including opening apps, finding your phone, and toggling Bluetooth or wifi).
